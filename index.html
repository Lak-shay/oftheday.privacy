OfTheDay Account and Data Deletion

This page explains how users of OfTheDay can request deletion of their account and associated data.

How to Request Deletion

To request deletion of your OfTheDay account and associated data, email us at privacy@yourdomain.com using the email address linked to your account.

Please include the following details in your request:

  • Your Google account email address linked to OfTheDay
  • Your OfTheDay user ID, if available

What We Delete

  • Your account profile information
  • Your gameplay history
  • Your leaderboard-related account data
  • Other app data associated with your account, where deletion is permitted

What We May Retain

Some limited information may be retained where necessary for legitimate business or legal reasons, including:

  • Purchase, transaction, or tax-related records
  • Fraud-prevention and security logs
  • Data we are required to retain by law

Retention Period

  • Deletion requests are generally processed within 30 days
  • Limited retained records may be kept for up to 90 days or longer if legally required
Important: Replace privacy@yourdomain.com with your real privacy contact email before publishing this page.
